Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Chefensa?
Chefensa is open to everyone who enjoys cooking.

Do I need any cooking qualification to join?
No, you could be a professional chef or a home maker who just loves cooking. Your passion for cooking is the only criteria.

How will I join Chefensa?
Give us a call or drop a mail. Tell us your specialty and what you love to cook. We will get in touch with you with our on boarding formalities.

Where and when do I have to cook?
You will cook from the convenience of your home at time mutually suitable for both of us.

Do I need to cook daily?
No, you just have to tell us the day before about your availability.

What quantity I have to cook?
We do not have a minimum or maximum cap on the quantity of food as long as it matches our quality parameters and customer requirements. You can enlist as less as 3 meals or as high as 50 meals on Chefensa.

What benefits I will get?
Apart from the monetary benefits, you will get appreciation and fame for your culinary skills.
